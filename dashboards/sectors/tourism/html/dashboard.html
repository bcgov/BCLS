Sector Definition

B.C. Tourism Sector Dashboard
The British Columbia tourism sector encompasses transportation services, accommodation providers, food and beverage services, recreation and entertainment activities, and travel-related services that support domestic and international visitors. Tourism is a major contributor to the B.C. economy, supporting businesses, employment, and regional development across the province.

Reference: SME Profile: Tourism Industries in Canada, 2020.
