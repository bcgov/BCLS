Summer surge: July–August account for 29% of total annual visitors, peaking at 1.22M in July.
Land dominates year-round: Land crossings (3.86M, 46%) lead all modes; Water (ferry) is highly seasonal — 85% of water arrivals occur May–Sep.
Australia leads international: Australia tops BC's non-US market with 142K visitors in 2025, followed by the UK (141K) and China (133K).
2nd nationally: BC ranks 2nd among provinces in 2025 with 8.36M visitors for the full year, behind Ontario (13.7M).
